ATV TOUR INTRODUCTION :

Thank you for choosing Skyway Travel. We’re happy to have you with us
for this tour through Cappadocia’s valleys.

Important note. Our guide’s English might not be perfect, but please
make sure to follow our guide’s instructions at all times. To get the
most out of the experience, we also recommend using the Cappadocia
Valleys audio guide. Just ask our guide which valley you are visiting,
then play the matching part of the audio guide we’ve shared. This way,
you can enjoy the history and stories while you’re right there in the
valley.

Here’s what’s included in your tour. Helmets and riding caps are
included for your safety, and also to protect you from the dust along
the way. Our guide will lead you to the valley where the balloons
usually launch over the fairy chinmeys. In every valley, we give you a
ten-minute break so you can stretch, walk around, and take photos. The
one-hour tour goes through two valleys. The two-hour tour covers three
valleys.

Enjoy your ride and do not forget to listen Cappadocia Valleys Audio
Guides.
