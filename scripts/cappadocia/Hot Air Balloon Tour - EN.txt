Hot Air Balloon Tour – Introduction

Good morning, and thank you for choosing Skyway Travel. Today, you are
about to experience one of the most iconic journeys in the world — a hot
air balloon flight over Cappadocia’s valleys and fairy chimneys.

Important note about weather conditions:

Hot air balloon flights are fully regulated by Turkish Civil Aviation,
and safety is always the first priority. Sometimes, due to weather
conditions, flights may be cancelled. In certain cases, this decision is
made the day before, while in other situations it can only be confirmed
early in the morning at the launch site. Please understand that this is
completely beyond the control of the balloon companies, as it is a
matter of national aviation safety.

If your flight is cancelled, you will always receive a full refund.
Alternatively, if availability allows, we will gladly reschedule you for
the following day. However, during the high season this is often not
possible, since the next day’s flights are usually fully booked well in
advance, just like today’s flight.

Now, let us explain the experience ahead.

Each basket carries up to 28 or 32 passengers, depending on the balloon.
The basket is divided into four compartments, each designed to hold four
passengers comfortably. Please remain in your assigned compartment
during the entire flight to keep the balloon balanced and safe.

On board, you will always find two licensed pilots: a main pilot in full
command of the flight, and a second pilot acting as observer and
support. They constantly monitor the weather, altitude, and landing
conditions. Please follow their instructions carefully at all times.

A balloon has no steering wheel. Its course depends entirely on the
wind. The pilots control altitude by heating or cooling the air inside
the envelope, rising or descending to catch different air currents. This
allows them to guide the balloon, but the direction always depends on
nature. There is no need to suggest routes — trust the pilots and the
wind, which together make each flight unique.

The planned flight time is around 60 minutes, but depending on
conditions, it may be shorter. Civil aviation rules prioritize safety.
If weather changes, the pilots may land earlier than expected. You may
also see some balloons still on the ground while others are already
flying. This is part of the civil aviation system, where balloons are
cleared for launch in sequence to keep skies safe. Once permission is
given, our crew is ready to take off immediately.

The most important instruction you will hear is “Landing position!”.
When the pilot calls this command:

– Bend your knees slightly,

– Hold the handles inside the basket firmly,

– Face forward,

– Keep your head low until the pilot confirms it is safe to stand.

This protects you during landing, which may be soft or may include a
short bump or slide.

During the flight, do not lean over or sit on the basket’s edge. Keep
your belongings secure, especially phones and cameras. Smoking is
strictly prohibited.

Throughout the journey, you will see Cappadocia’s valleys beneath you.
If you ask the pilot which valley you are flying over, you can select
the matching audio guide and listen — as if a professional guide were
right beside you. Each valley has its own history, legends, and
atmosphere, making your flight even more meaningful.

Finally, please note that photos and videos taken during preparations or
in flight are not included in the tour. These are offered individually,
and if you wish, you may purchase them directly from the pilot or
photographer for an additional fee.

On behalf of Skyway Travel, we thank you once again and wish you a safe
and unforgettable flight.
