Camel Ride Introduction

Thank you for choosing Skyway Travel. We’re happy to have you with us
for this camel ride through Cappadocia’s valleys.

Important note. Our guide’s English might not be perfect, but please
always follow our guide’s instructions. To learn more about the valleys,
ask our guide where you are, then play the matching part of the
Cappadocia Valleys audio guide. This way, you can enjoy the history and
stories while you’re right there in the valley.

Here’s what to expect. The one-hour tour goes through Meskendir Valley
with a photo stop. The ninety-minute tour takes you to Güllüdere Valley,
also with a photo stop. Sunrise and sunset tours last ninety minutes,
while most daytime tours are one hour. Sometimes, before sunset, daytime
tours can also be ninety minutes.

Our camels walk at the same pace as a person, and our team walks
alongside them for safety. No special clothing is needed — just dress
for the weather. All camels are cared for with love and checked
regularly by veterinarians. Their health and happiness are always a
priority for us.

Children are welcome. From one year old, they can ride with parents, and
anyone who can sit properly, hold on, and follow instructions can ride
on their own at any age.

Free services include transfers from nearby hotels, colorful disposable
hats, and photos or videos during the ride by your phones.

Drone filming is available depending on the weather, with an extra
charge.
