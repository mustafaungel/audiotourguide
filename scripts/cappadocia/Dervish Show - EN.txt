Good evening, and thank you for choosing Skyway Travel. Tonight, before
we enter, we would like to share some important background about the
ceremony you are about to witness — the Whirling Dervishes, or Sema.

The Mevlevi Order was founded in the 13th century in Konya by the
followers of Mevlânâ Jalaluddin Rumi, the great poet and Sufi
philosopher. His message of love, tolerance, and devotion inspired this
ritual. What you will see is not entertainment, not a dance performance
— it is worship. Every gesture, every sound, every turn is full of
spiritual meaning.

Look at the dervishes’ clothing. The tall felt hat, called sikke,
represents a tombstone. The black cloak, which is removed at the
beginning, symbolizes the grave. Underneath is the white skirt, the
tennure, representing the shroud — the death of the ego. When the
dervish begins to turn, he symbolically leaves behind the material world
and seeks closeness to the divine.

Notice the hands. The right hand is raised to the heavens to receive
blessings, the left hand is turned toward the earth to share them with
creation. In this way, the dervish becomes a bridge between heaven and
earth.

The ceremony itself has stages. It begins with poetry in praise of the
Prophet, then the sound of the ney flute, which expresses the longing of
the soul. The dervishes walk in circles to greet one another, before the
turning begins. The heart of the ritual is the Four Selams, or
greetings, each representing a step toward God: awareness of His
greatness, recognition of His power, surrender of the ego, and finally
union with the divine.

The ceremony closes with a prayer for peace — for prophets, for
believers, and for all humanity. The music of the flute, the drum, and
the hymns, together with the sight of the turning dervishes, create an
atmosphere beyond time.

Please keep in mind, silence is essential once we enter. Photography is
usually not allowed inside, in order to respect the sacred atmosphere.

We hope this introduction helps you understand what you are about to
see. May this evening leave a lasting memory in your heart. And once
again, thank you for choosing Skyway Travel.
