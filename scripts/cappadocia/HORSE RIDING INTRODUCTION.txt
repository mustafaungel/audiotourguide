HORSE RIDING INTRODUCTION;
Thank you for choosing Skyway Travel. We hope you enjoy a unique
horseback riding experience through Cappadocia’s valleys. Important
note, your guide’s English may be limited. That’s why, after this
introduction, we suggest you also listen to the Cappadocia Valleys audio
guide.

During the tour, simply ask your guide which valley you are in, then
play the matching part of the audio guide we shared with you. This way,
you can enjoy the full history and stories of the valleys you see. The
one-hour ride takes you through two valleys with a stop for photos. The
two-hour ride covers at least three valleys, with two breaks, one for
photos and the other at a café where you can rest and enjoy drinks.

If this is your first time on a horse, don’t worry. The horses are calm,
the pace is easy, and the ride is smooth, so it is safe even for
beginners. Guests who want a faster gallop should book a private
one-hour or two-hour ride. Clothing is important; leggings or trousers
are best, dresses are not suitable.

The age limit is between seven and sixty-four years, and if you have
allergies to animals, dust, or fur, this tour is not suitable. Pick-up
and drop-off are arranged by the ranch according to your hotel, and you
should be ready at the hotel reception on time. At the end of the ride,
you will be offered a cold drink. Helmets are provided for all guests,
and protective vests are available for children when needed.
